Designed Specifically for Teens

Luneya isn't a generic social app with age settings bolted on. Every feature, every design decision, every policy was built with a 15-year-old in mind — their safety, their emotional wellbeing, and their need for genuine connection.

Getting Started

Three simple steps
to a real connection

No follower counts. No algorithms. Just you, your interests, and one conversation worth having.

1

Create Your Profile

Choose a username, pick your age group, and select 5–10 genuine interests. No real name needed. No photo required. Your identity is yours to protect.

2

Match on Shared Interests

Luneya finds someone in your age range who shares at least three of your interests. No browsing strangers. No swiping. A thoughtful match — not a feed.

3

Build One Real Conversation

You get one active chat. Put your whole self into it. If it clicks, you can choose to share how to stay in touch. If not, you can end it and match again. No pressure.

Why Luneya Exists

Teens today are more
connected — and lonelier
than ever

The average teenager sends hundreds of messages a day. They have thousands of followers. They're constantly online. And yet study after study shows Gen Z feels deeply disconnected from real relationships.

Mainstream social media optimises for engagement — likes, reactions, virality. It rewards performance over presence. It replaces depth with volume.

Luneya is the opposite. It's designed to create the conditions for one genuine conversation — and get out of the way.
